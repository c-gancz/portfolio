About Me

Hello there! Specializing primarily in print and web design, I’m trained as a professional graphic designer, having graduated from a bachelor’s degree in Design at Concordia University and having worked as a graphic designer both in freelance and as an employee. Having additionally graduated with a minor in History, I’m very interested in undergoing extensive research prior to making design work, making my projects very diverse and conceptual.
